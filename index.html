About Me

Oorja Mehra was born in India and moved to the USA as a baby with her family. She lived prmarily on the East Coast, moving from Tampa, FL to Mahopac, NY when she was in middle school. She got her Bachelor's Degree in Literature from American University in Washington, DC. From there, Oorja took her time exploring her passions and interests, looking for the perfect creative outlet and searching for a career rather than a job.

When she was 27, Oorja moved to St. Louis, MO to be with her fiance and got a job working in HR at Olin Business School at Wash U. It was there that she discovered her path to coding. She took Wash U's Coding Bootcamp while continuing her work in HR and realized that this was the perfect field for her: it combined creativity with logic and structure. As they say, the rest is herstory.
